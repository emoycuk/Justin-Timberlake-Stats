Timberlake Analytics

A fan-built real-time statistics platform

What Is This?

Timberlake Analytics is an independently built, data-driven fan site tracking Justin Timberlake's career in real time — album sales, streaming statistics, record certifications, box office grosses, and awards history, all in one place.

Every number on this site is sourced from publicly available data and updated continuously. This is not an official Justin Timberlake or RCA Records project.

Methodology — CSPC

Career Sales Performance Chart (CSPC) is a methodology developed by Chartmasters to convert all music consumption formats into a single unified metric: Equivalent Album Sales (EAS).

The following conversion formulas are applied across all eras:

Source Formula Notes
Pure Album Sales × 1 Direct unit count — global sales
Physical Singles sales × 3/10 3 singles ≈ 1 album equivalent
Download Singles sales × 1.5/10 Lower weight due to lower price point
Audio Streams (streams × 1.82) ÷ 1166 1.82 = artist ratio (catalog/total); 1166 streams ≈ 1 EAS
Video Streams views ÷ 6750 YouTube — lower weight than audio streams
US streaming share is adjusted by era: pre-2016 catalog uses 27% US share (lower global penetration at release time); post-2016 uses 35%. This affects RIAA live eligibility calculations on the Certifications page.

Certified Units vs. Pure Sales

These are two different metrics that are often confused. Justin Timberlake's record label reports approximately 117 million pure sales — meaning physical and digital purchases only, with no streaming counted.

However, modern certification bodies (RIAA since 2016, BPI since 2014, ARIA since 2016, and others) count streaming equivalents toward certifications: in the US, 150 streams = 1 single sale and 1,500 streams = 1 album sale. When all certifications are aggregated across RIAA, BPI, ARIA, Music Canada, and 60+ other certifying bodies, Justin Timberlake's total reaches over 170 million certified units.

Neither number is "wrong" — they measure different things. Pure sales reflect purchases. Certified units reflect the industry's current standard for measuring consumption, which includes streaming.

Why This Site Differs From ChartMasters

ChartMasters is the original publisher of the CSPC methodology and an authoritative source for career sales estimates. Timberlake Analytics builds on that foundation with a different focus: Justin Timberlake exclusively, with deeper per-era and per-certification breakdowns not available on a multi-artist platform.

RIAA Live Eligibility

The Certifications page shows both official RIAA certifications and a live eligibility estimate computed in real time from current streams and sales data. When the live estimate exceeds the official certification (which may not have been updated), the higher value is displayed.

RIAA album formula: (US streams ÷ 1500) + pure sales + (track sales ÷ 10). RIAA single formula: (US streams ÷ 150) + pure sales.
