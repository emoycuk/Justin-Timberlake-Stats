Justin Timberlake Album Sales & Career Statistics

Justin Timberlake is one of the best-selling music artists of all time. His record label reports 117 million pure sales worldwide (physical + digital, no streaming). Across all certifying bodies (RIAA, BPI, ARIA, and 60+ others), his total reaches over 170 million certified units — a figure that includes streaming equivalents counted by modern certification standards. This site's real-time Equivalent Album Sales (EAS) calculation, which combines physical sales, digital sales, and Spotify/YouTube streaming equivalents using industry-standard formulas, currently shows — EAS.

Album Sales

  • Justified (2002): over 12 million copies sold worldwide. US: 3x Platinum (RIAA). UK: 2x Platinum.
  • FutureSex/LoveSounds (2006): over 20 million copies sold worldwide. US: 4x Platinum (RIAA). UK: 5x Platinum. Best-selling album.
  • The 20/20 Experience (2013): over 10 million copies sold worldwide. US: 4x Platinum (RIAA).
  • The 20/20 Experience – 2 of 2 (2013): US Platinum (RIAA).
  • Man of the Woods (2018): US Gold (RIAA).
  • Everything I Thought It Was (2024): most recent studio album.

Streaming Statistics

Justin Timberlake has over 17 billion total Spotify streams. Top streamed songs:

  • Can't Stop the Feeling! — over 2 billion Spotify streams
  • Mirrors — over 1.5 billion Spotify streams
  • SexyBack — over 1 billion Spotify streams
  • What Goes Around...Comes Around — over 800 million Spotify streams
  • Cry Me a River — over 700 million Spotify streams

Certifications

Justin Timberlake has earned RIAA certifications: Justified 3x Platinum USA, FutureSex/LoveSounds 4x Platinum USA, The 20/20 Experience 4x Platinum USA. Additional certifications in UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Brazil, and 60+ countries.

Tour Box Office

  • The 20/20 Experience World Tour (2013–2014): grossed over $200 million
  • Man of the Woods Tour (2018–2019): grossed over $200 million
  • Forget Tomorrow World Tour (2024–2025): ongoing

Awards

Justin Timberlake has won 10 Grammy Awards, 4 Emmy Awards, and numerous MTV VMAs and American Music Awards.
