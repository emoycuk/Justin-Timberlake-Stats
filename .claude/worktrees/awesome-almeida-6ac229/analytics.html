Deep Analytics

Cross-dimensional career analysis

Era Power Index
Each era is scored across 6 dimensions — Streaming, Certifications, Chart Performance, Tour Revenue, Awards, and Longevity — then normalized to a 0-100 scale. Click an era in the legend to toggle visibility.
Methodology: Streaming = total Spotify streams (Kworb). Certifications = sum of RIAA/BPI/ARIA/Canada platinum-equivalent units. Charts = weighted Hot 100 score (inverse peak × weeks on chart). Tours = box office gross attributed to era. Awards = Grammy + Emmy + Billboard + VMA + AMA wins. Longevity = total weeks on Billboard 200. All metrics normalized per-dimension to 0-100 where 100 = best performing era.
Hit Anatomy
Every charting single mapped by Billboard Hot 100 peak position (Y-axis, inverted — #1 is top) vs. weeks on chart (X-axis). Bubble size represents total Spotify streams. Hover for details.
Data: Billboard Hot 100 peak & weeks from chart history. Spotify streams from Kworb (latest snapshot). Bubble radius scales logarithmically from stream count. Singles without Spotify data shown at minimum size.
First Week Sales
US first-week album sales (Billboard 200). The 20/20 Experience holds JT's biggest debut at 968K — the largest first week of 2013.
Source: Billboard 200 first-week figures (SoundScan / Luminate). Pure album sales + TEA + SEA where applicable.
